Facel Véga previews the HK-500-E concept sedan

Topic: News

Publication date: 2026-01-22

Source: https://www.reuters.com/

Facel Véga has outlined a revival plan centered on the HK-500-E, a new electric grand tourer concept presented in Paris this week. The company said the design draws on classic long-hood proportions while integrating a clean-sheet EV platform.

Executives described a limited production strategy aimed at collectors and boutique retailers, with the first customer deliveries targeted for mid‑2027. The brand said it is in late-stage supplier talks for battery modules and interior components.

Early targets include a 600-kilometer WLTP range, sub‑4‑second 0–100 km/h acceleration, and fast-charging capability under 20 minutes for a 10–80% session. The company plans to share a full technical specification later this year.
