Coastal City FC outlines a youth training overhaul

Topic: Sport

Publication date: 2026-01-22

Source: https://apnews.com/

Coastal City FC has announced a three‑year overhaul of its academy system, aiming to improve player development and retention. The club said the changes will begin this summer with a restructured coaching hierarchy and a refreshed curriculum for every age group.

The program adds two full‑time sports scientists, a dedicated goalkeeper unit, and a partnership with a local university to provide academic support for under‑18 squads. A new mentorship track will pair academy players with senior team professionals during preseason blocks.

Officials said the academy will introduce standardized fitness testing, a revised match‑analysis process, and enhanced support for player welfare. The club also plans to expand scouting in regional leagues to improve recruitment coverage.

Club executives said the investment is intended to reduce reliance on transfer spending and create a clearer pathway from the academy to the first team over the next two seasons.
