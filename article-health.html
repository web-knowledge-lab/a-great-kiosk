Regional clinic pilots a mobile screening program

Topic: Health

Publication date: 2026-01-22

Source: https://www.who.int/

A regional clinic has launched a mobile screening program aimed at improving early detection rates in rural areas. The initiative will rotate across six towns on a weekly schedule.

Health officials said the pilot focuses on preventive care and aims to reduce travel time for patients who face long distances to reach primary services.

If the program meets participation targets, the clinic plans to expand the route and add evening appointments.
