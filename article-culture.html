Museum program spotlights community archives

Topic: Culture

Publication date: 2026-01-22

Source: https://www.loc.gov/

A city museum has launched a program to digitize community archives, focusing on neighborhood collections and oral histories. The initiative will begin with a pilot archive submitted by local residents.

Curators said the project aims to preserve materials that are often missing from official records, including photographs, flyers, and personal journals.

Selected materials will be made available through an open online catalog later this year.
