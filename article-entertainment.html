Indie studio announces a stop-motion feature

Topic: Entertainment

Publication date: 2026-01-22

Source: https://www.oscars.org/

An independent animation studio has green‑lit a stop‑motion feature scheduled to begin production this fall. The project will be shot on handcrafted miniature sets and is expected to take 18 months to complete.

Producers said the film will premiere on the festival circuit before a limited theatrical release. A composer has been hired to deliver an original orchestral score.

The studio plans to release a short behind‑the‑scenes series during production to showcase the animation process.
