Open hardware collective debuts a repairable tablet

Topic: Tech

Publication date: 2026-01-22

Source: https://www.ifixit.com/

An open‑hardware collective has unveiled a repairable tablet designed around modular components and long‑term support. The company said the display, battery, and storage are all user‑replaceable with standard tools.

Early prototypes are being tested with developer partners, and the group plans to publish full service documentation ahead of a limited production run.

Pricing and final specifications are expected later this year, alongside a commitment to multi‑year software updates.
