Central bank signals gradual path for rates

Topic: Finance

Publication date: 2026-01-22

Source: https://www.ecb.europa.eu/

The central bank signaled a gradual approach to rate changes, citing easing inflation and mixed growth indicators. Officials said they will continue to watch wage trends and energy prices before committing to a faster policy shift.

In a statement following its monthly meeting, the bank noted that consumer demand has softened and that credit conditions remain tight. Markets interpreted the language as a signal that the first move may come later in the year.
